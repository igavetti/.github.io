About Me

I am originally from northern Italy and am currently pursuing a Research Master’s in Brain and Cognitive Sciences at the University of Amsterdam. My background spans Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on computational neuroscience and machine/deep learning. My goal is to contribute to advancing our understanding of brain function and artificial intelligence.
